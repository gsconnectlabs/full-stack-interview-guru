# FullStackInterviewGuru (FIG) - Project Context

## Project Overview

FullStackInterviewGuru (FIG) is a premium interview preparation platform designed to help software engineers prepare for technical interviews through high-quality explanations, structured learning paths, AI-assisted learning, and real-world interview knowledge.

This is NOT another interview question website.

Our mission is to become one of the world's most trusted interview preparation platforms.

---

# Vision

Build the most trusted interview preparation platform that developers genuinely enjoy using.

We believe trust comes before monetization.

Users should feel confident recommending FIG to others because of its quality, clarity, and professionalism.
